# Tier Ranking

## Current Tier

**Tier 4: strong beta**

## Why It Is Tier 4

### What makes it good

- The product works.
- The architecture is coherent.
- The core use cases are relevant.
- Several isolated formulas and benchmark oracles are exactly right.
- The app has enough UX quality and enough methodological clarity to be genuinely useful.

### What would make it excellent

- removing trust failures
- expanding scenario realism
- hardening outputs for real-world communication
- making the simulation and waterfall layers more honest and more precise

### What would make it world class

- institutional-grade term modeling
- presentation-grade outputs
- fund-level and portfolio-level coverage
- premium analytical design
- deep pedagogical and decision-support polish

## Why It Is Not Tier 5: Professional Grade

Professional-grade software cannot have:

- contradictory capital inputs
- mislabeled outputs
- weak export surfaces
- incomplete trust signaling
- major unsupported scenarios hidden inside a polished interface

This app still has all five.

## Why It Is Not Tier 6: Elite

Elite software in this category would need:

- fund-aware venture realism
- per-series preferred realism
- institutional reporting and exports
- stronger validation discipline
- broader complaint resistance from founders, VCs, and educators

This app is not close enough yet.

## Why It Is Not Tier 7: World Class

World class requires:

- no major trust failures
- no obvious venture naivete
- no output embarrassment
- no serious mathematical or product ambiguity
- strong elegance in both logic and interface

The app is disqualified on trustworthiness, shareability, and venture realism.

### What makes it good

### What would make it excellent

- Lifting finance correctness, shareability, and trustworthiness above the “caveat-heavy” zone.

- No severe trust failures.

## Does Any Score Disqualify It From World Class?

**Yes.**

The following are disqualifying for any world-class claim:

- Shareability `4.2`
- Trustworthiness `5.2`
- Finance correctness `5.8`
- Venture realism `5.5`

Even if the UI and pedagogy improved dramatically, those categories alone would keep the product below world-class status.

## Final Tier Justification

This app is not a toy, not a novelty, and not merely competent. It is more serious than that. But it is still a **strong beta** rather than a professional-grade platform because it lacks the trust, depth, and communication quality required for elite users to rely on it without complaint.
